Good things to do while sitting at home in COVID

Cleaning first! Clean your closet and through all things that you don't need. Anything that you didn't touch for a year or more, get rid of it without regrets. This life too preciouses to keep unnecessary things; and remember, all these cool recipes that take too much time, there is never time to spend hours on the kitchen. Now you can cook this delicious brownies or cake of your dream and don’t worry to gain weight, who cares about it if you can die tomorrow.

Decorating! Finally if you had something in your mind to make for your house but didn’t have time to research, don't miss this opportunity to make something special using your imagination. Have a lot fun! This is a perfect time to have a lot of fun without worrying that there is no time. If you never had chance just to sit and play video game or be foolish with your kids playing around and do some messy things, this is the time!
